The problem

Swipe apps optimize for the wrong thing.

They make money when you stay. So they reward swiping, not meeting. You get a feed that never ends, a pile of matches that never message, and a calendar that stays empty.

11hThe average dating-app user spends nearly 11 hours a week swiping and messaging.
~50%About half of matches never turn into a single message, let alone a date.
#1Ghosting is now the default exit, not the exception. Burnout follows.
How it works

Four steps. One real date.

1

A 2-minute AI intake

We learn your intent, values, and pace. No 200-field questionnaire.

2

3

Hear each other before you commit to meeting. Tone says what text can't.

4

Skip straight to a date

We help you pick a time and a place, fast. Offline is the whole point.
