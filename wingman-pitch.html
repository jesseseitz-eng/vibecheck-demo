The shift

Dating is becoming entertainment.

Love Island and Love Is Blind are cultural events. Dating creators pull millions of views filming real dates. Livestream tipping turned watching into a paid, interactive sport. Three waves are colliding, and nobody has built the product in the middle.

📺

Reality dating

The most-watched format on streaming. People are obsessed with watching strangers fall for each other.

🎥

Dating creators

Creators film real dates for huge audiences, but have no native way to monetize the moment.

🪙

Live tipping

Twitch and TikTok proved audiences will pay, live, to influence what happens on screen.

The insight

Two broken things, one product.

💔

Dating apps can't grow

Swipe fatigue is peaking, the big apps are shrinking, and acquisition is brutally expensive. The product is also boring to watch.

🍿

Watching dates is content gold

Audiences love it and creators already make it. Wingman turns the date itself into an interactive, monetizable live show.

Wingman is not a better way to get matched. It is a new way to watch, fund, and direct other people's dates, with daters and creators getting paid to show up.

Guardrails

Built consensual, on purpose.

  • +Opt-in everything. Daters choose to be on, and can pass or veto any play, instantly, with refunds.
  • +18+ and verified. ID and identity checks for everyone who appears on stream.
  • +No buying access to a person. You influence the show, never contact or control a participant directly. Live moderation throughout.
  • +Currency, not a casino. Wings buy moments and status, not bets on outcomes, keeping us clear of gambling rules.
